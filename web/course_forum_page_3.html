Great suggestions, David! To add to that, think of it like this: an electron is like a shy person at a party. When no one is looking, they're dancing all over the place (wave-like behavior). But the moment you look at them (measure their position), they stop and stand in one spot (particle-like behavior). It's a simplification, but it helped me grasp the core idea. Don't worry, you'll get there!
